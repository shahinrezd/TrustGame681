Instructions/Task 1

You have been randomly and anonymously paired with another participant. One of you will be selected at random to be participant A; the other will be participant B. You will learn whether you are participant A or B prior to making any decision.

To start, participant A receives {{ Constants.endowment }}; participant B receives nothing. Participant A can send some or all of their {{ Constants.endowment }} to participant B. Before B receives this amount, it will be multiplied by {{ Constants.multiplier }}. Once informed of the amount sent to them, B can decide to send some, or all of it to back to A. You will be asked to complete this task 5 times. You will be randomly paired each time. There is no guarantee that you will be paired with the same person again.

After the experiment is finished, you will asked to complete a short questionnaire. As a thank you for completing this experiment you will be put in a draw to randomly win a prize.
